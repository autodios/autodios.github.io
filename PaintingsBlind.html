"Paola Torres builds what I call a knot of code, and plays with the frontier
of understanding through the different senses; For example, you can be non-deaf, but you can hear the sound,
however you do not understand it, you can be blind and you can understand the braille, but you do not 
understand the sound either ... Actually it is a game of many codes included in one single machine. 
Braille immediately refers to how the senses can limit the perception of certain things of reality. "
